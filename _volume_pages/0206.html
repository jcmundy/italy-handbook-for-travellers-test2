{% raw %}mistress of the world, the city rapidly increased. The wall was al¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}amassed his fortune; for rents were high, and the houses of a slight{% endraw %}

{% raw %}and luxury. Thus, for example, the tribune Clodius, the well-{% endraw %}

{% raw %}14,800,600 sesterces (i. e. about 130,5251.). During the last cen¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}unpaved, were now converted into the massive lava-causeways{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Appia). The highest ambition of the opulent nobles was to per¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}petuate their names by the erection of imposing public buildings.{% endraw %}
