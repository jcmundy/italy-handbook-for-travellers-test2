{% raw %}The library was neglected and dispersed by his successors. Sixtus IV.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was the first to revive the institution ; he assigned a locality under the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Sistine Chapel for the collection, appointed Platina (1475) director, and set{% endraw %}
{% raw %}apart definite revenues for its maintenance. Thus endowed, it increased stead¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ily, and the allotted space became more and more inadequate to its require¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ments, until in 1588 Sixtus V. caused the present magnificent edifice to be{% endraw %}
{% raw %}erected by Domenico Fontana, intersecting the great court of Bramante. To this{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ever-increasing collection several considerable libraries have been added by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}purchase or donation, some of which are catalogued and preserved separately.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}In 1623 the Elector Maximilian presented to the Pope the Bibliotheca Pala-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tina of Heidelberg, when the town was taken in the Thirty Years' War; and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in 1657 the Bibl. Urbinas, founded by Duke Federigo da Montefeltro, in 1690{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the B. Reginensis, once the property of Queen Christina of Sweden, and in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1746 the B. Ottoboniana, purchased by Alex. VIII. (Ottobuoni), were added.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Most of the MSS. carried off by the French were restored in 1814.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Vatican Library now contains nearly 24,000 MSS., of which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}about 17,400 are Latin, 3450 Greek, and 2000 Oriental. Of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}latter a printed catalogue has been published (1756-59), and contin¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ued by Card. Mai. There are also about 50,000 printed hooks, but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}those only which formerly belonged to the library of Card. Mai are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}catalogued and available for consultation. The principal librarian{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is a cardinal, at present Pitra, who in ordinary business is repre¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sented by the under-librarian, Monsignor Pecci (brother of Pope{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Leo XIII.), and the Custodian, Padre Bollich; besides these there are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}seven scrittori and several subordinate officials (scopatori). The use¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fulness of the library is greatly circumscribed by the numerous holi¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}days on which it is closed. It is not available on more than 100{% endraw %}
{% raw %}days in the year (from the middle of Nov. to the middle of June),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and then only for 3 hrs. daily (8-11). Permission to use it is best{% endraw %}
{% raw %}obtained through the traveller's ambassador, or by private letters of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}introduction, the applicant stating the branch of study contemplated.{% endraw %}
