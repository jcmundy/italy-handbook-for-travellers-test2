{% raw %}Painting. An altogether deplorable interval now ensued, during{% endraw %}

{% raw %}ming ascendency into a slavish imitativeness, content with the least{% endraw %}
{% raw %}possible effort to crowd into a given space the greatest possible{% endraw %}
{% raw %}numberof unmeaning figures, not devoid, however, of a certain super¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ficial charm sufficient to captivate the eye. After an interval thus{% endraw %}
