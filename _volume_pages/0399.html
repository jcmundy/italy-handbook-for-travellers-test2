{% raw %}1508, and, if the exaggerated account of his two biographers be be¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}hand, the Florentine assistants he had at first engaged having been{% endraw %}
{% raw %}speedily dismissed. Whether the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel, or{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Stanze of Raphael are to he regarded as the culminating effort{% endraw %}

{% raw %}of uniformity of thought and compactness of composition, must be{% endraw %}
