{% raw %}These ancient buildings were restored for the last time in the reign{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of king Theodoric, in the first half of the 6th century, and the last new{% endraw %}
{% raw %}monument erected in the Forum was the Column of Phocas, dating from{% endraw %}
{% raw %}608, but the rudeness of the architecture distinctly betrays the degraded{% endraw %}
{% raw %}taste of the period. As early indeed as the first half of the 6th cent, had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}begun the war of extermination waged by the Middle Ages against{% endraw %}
{% raw %}paganism. Ancient temples were transformed into churches, such as those{% endraw %}

{% raw %}SS. Sergio e Bacco in the temple of Concord and another at the S.E.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}corner of the Basilica Julia, have entirely disappeared. Interspersed with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}these churches were the towers and castles of the Roman nobility, erected{% endraw %}
{% raw %}among the ruins of the ancient buildings in the style best adapted for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the prosecution of their perpetual feuds. In most cases, the dimensions{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the monuments of antiquity, were far too vast to admit of their being{% endraw %}
{% raw %}used for mediaeval purposes, but another mode of utilising these immense{% endraw %}
{% raw %}masses of building materials readily suggested itself. Throughout a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thousand years the edifices of ancient Rome were employed as quarries,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from which churches and secular buildings alike derived their columns,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}their blocks of solid stone, and, owing to a still more destructive pro¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ceeding, their supplies of lime also from tlie burning of marble. The fact{% endraw %}

{% raw %}stone-masons' yards at three different places will convey an idea of the vast{% endraw %}
{% raw %}quantity of marble, bearing valuable inscriptions and artistic enrichments,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which must have been destroyed in this way; and it need hardly be observed{% endraw %}
