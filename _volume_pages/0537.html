{% raw %}List of Streets in the Plan of Rome.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The plan is divided into three sections, the upper,' with green margin,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}numbered I, the central, white, II, the lower, with red margin, III. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}three columns of figures in the subjoined list correspond to these sections ;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the numbers indicate the square of the section in which the place in question{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is to be found (thus: Accademia di S. Luca is in the 14th square of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1st section). Where space has been too limited to admit of the names{% endraw %}
{% raw %}being inserted in the plan, they have been replaced by numbers, which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the following list are annexed to each name so omitted (thus: Banco{% endraw %}
{% raw %}di S. Spirito i5 is No. 15 in the 10th square of the 2nd section). The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}key to these numbers in their order is also inserted in the plan itself, an{% endraw %}
{% raw %}arrangement which will often be found useful.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Abbreviations: V. = Via, Vic. = Vicolo, Vg. = Vigna, Pal. = Palazzo.{% endraw %}
