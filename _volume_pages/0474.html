{% raw %}Frascati, in a cool and healthy situation, on the slope of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mountains, with its beautiful, shady, and well-watered villas, is a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}favourite summer-resort of foreigners as well as natives. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ancient Tusculum having been destroyed by the Romans in 1191,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}this town, which is insignificant and comparatively modern, sprang{% endraw %}
{% raw %}up on the ruins of a former villa, overgrown with underwood{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(fretsche), from which it derives its name. In the piazza, which is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}embellished with a pretty fountain, rises the cathedral of S. Pietro,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}erected in 1700 under Innocent XII. To the left of the high-altar{% endraw %}
