{% raw %}The finest views of the falls are commanded by the new road{% endraw %}
{% raw %}itself. Or, before reaching the falls, we may ascend a path to the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}left, leading in 10 min. to a small summer-house, affording the{% endraw %}

{% raw %}to us. — We now return to the road, and retracing our steps to the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}first path on the left, cross the Nera by a natural bridge, below{% endraw %}

{% raw %}the left is to be selected. The surrounding rocks (in which there{% endraw %}
