{% raw %}Porto d'Anzio (Trattoria di Ambrogio Pollastrini; private apart¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ments procurable) is a very pleasant place in the early summer,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}but fever begins in July, often with great suddenness.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Antium, the capital of the Volsci, and a prosperous seaport at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}an early period, the place where Coriolanus sought refuge when{% endraw %}
{% raw %}banished from Rome in B. C. 490, was compelled in 468 to suc¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cumb to the Romans. In 338, when all the Latins were conquered,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Antium received a Roman colony, and was thus permanently united{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with Rome. Extensive villas were afterwards erected here. Cicero{% endraw %}
{% raw %}possessed an estate at Antium, the tranquillity and charms of which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}he highly extols (Att. iv, 8). Horace (Carm. i, 35) mentions the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}temple of Fortune at the 'lovely Antium', where oracular responses{% endraw %}
{% raw %}were given, and which was consulted as late as the time of Theo-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dositts the Great (about 390). Claudius and Nero were born at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Antium, where the latter erected magnificent edifices. Domitian,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Hadrian, Antoninus Pius, and Lucius Verus resided at Antium in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}summer. The Goths and Saracens afterwards took up their quarters{% endraw %}
{% raw %}here. In the 14th cent, the place was entirely deserted, but in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the 16th the popes began to restore the harbour.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Since 1831 Porto d'Anzio and Nettuno have been the property{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Prince Borghese, whose handsome villa here is said to occupy{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the site of the ancient Arx. Extensive substructures, broken col¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}umns, etc. have been discovered. Under Julius II. the Apollo Bel¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vedere I and probably the Diana of Versailles also) was extricated{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from the ruins near the so-called Arco Muto, and at a later period{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Borghese Gladiator also (now in Paris). The town possesses{% endraw %}
{% raw %}beautiful villas of the Corsini (now Mencacci) and Doria families,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and a Bagno for convicts.{% endraw %}
