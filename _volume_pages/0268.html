{% raw %}Leaving S. Martino, we next proceed in 5 min. to —{% endraw %}
{% raw %}*S. Pietro in Vincoli (PL II, 23; 151 ft. above the sea-level),{% endraw %}
{% raw %}also named Basilica Eudoxiana after Eudoxia, wife of Valentinian{% endraw %}
{% raw %}III., who founded it, about 442, as a receptacle for the chains of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}St. Peter which had been presented by her to Pope Leo I. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}church was restored by Pelagius I. and Hadrian I., the vestibule{% endraw %}
{% raw %}added by Baccio Pintelli, and the whole is now modernised. It is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}open before 11 a. m. and after 3 p. m. ; when closed, visitors ring{% endraw %}
{% raw %}at the adjacent door to the left, No. 4 ('/2 fr.).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Interior. The nave and aisles are separated by 20 antique Doric{% endraw %}
{% raw %}columns. To the left of the entrance is the monument of the Florentine{% endraw %}
{% raw %}painters Pietro and Antonio Pollajuolo (d. 1498). The fresco above it,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}representing the plague of 680, is attributed to the latter master. The{% endraw %}

{% raw %}— At the end of the Right Aisle is the monument of Pope Julius II. with the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'■Statue of Moses by Michael Angelo, one of his most famous works. The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}monument was originally destined for St. Peter's, and intended to be a most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}imposing work, consisting of upwards of 30 statues. Owing to various ad¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}verse circumstances the portion preserved here was alone completed. (Two{% endraw %}
{% raw %}statues destined for this monument are at the Louvre.) The statues of Moses{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(who is represented by mediaeval Christian artists with horns owing to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}an erroneous translation of Exodus xxxiv. 35), Rachel, and Leah (as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}symbols, on the feft of meditative, on the right of active life) alone were{% endraw %}
{% raw %}executed by the great master, and even these were not entirely his own{% endraw %}
{% raw %}workmanship. The grouping only of the remainder was from his design.{% endraw %}
