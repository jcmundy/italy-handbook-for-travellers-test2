{% raw %}(30-4Oc). Mischio is a mixture of coffee and chocolate (20-30c), considered{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wholesome and nutritious.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}cafes at 30-90c. per portion; or a half portion (mezzo) may be ordered.{% endraw %}
