{% raw %}A portico of granite columns, one of which still remains, with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}marble entablature now supported by pillars of masonry, lay in front{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of these apartments. The walls are covered with writing (graffiti,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}done with the stilus, or ancient substitute for a pen), consisting of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}names, sentences , and sketches, similar to the performances of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mischief-loving schoolboys of the present day. The well-known{% endraw %}
