{% raw %}To the left, by the Scala Santa, is a tribune erected by Bene¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}the left, Christ enthroned delivers the keys to Pope Sylvester and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the banner to the Emp. Constantine ; on the right, St. Peter pre¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}senting the papal stole to Leo and the banner to Charlemagne. —{% endraw %}

{% raw %}the Aqua Claudia (p. 246). An avenue leads hence in 5 min. to{% endraw %}
