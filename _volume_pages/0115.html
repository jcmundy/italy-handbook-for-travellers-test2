{% raw %}Cortona, a small, loftily situated town with 9000 inhab. (with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}neighbouring villages 26,000), above the valley of the Chiana, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}not far from the Trasimene Lake, is one of the most ancient cities{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in Italy. Its situation and views, its Etruscan antiquities, and the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}number of good pictures it possesses, render it well worthy of a visit.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It appears that the Ktruscans, immigrating from the plain of the I'o,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wrested the place from the Umbrians , and constituted it their principal{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stronghold when they proceeded to extend their conquests in Etruri.-i.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cortona was one of the twelve confederate cities of Etruria, and with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}them shared the fate of being converted into aRoman colony. After various{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vicissitudes and struggles it came under the dominion of Florence in 1410.{% endraw %}
