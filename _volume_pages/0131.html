{% raw %}Carriage there and back, a drive of l'/2-2 hrs., 12 fr. — The most in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}teresting of the tombs, and one of the handsomest, though not oldest in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}N. Etruria, is the Sepolcro de' Volunni (the tomb of the Volumnii), close{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the road, where it is intersected by the railway. It consists of ten{% endraw %}
{% raw %}chambers, hewn in the coarse-grained tufa, of which the hill is composed,{% endraw %}
