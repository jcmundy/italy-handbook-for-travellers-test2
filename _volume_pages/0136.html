{% raw %}Renaissance, are by Giovanni Pisano (?), Andrea, and other pupils of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Niecold Pisano, and represent scenes from the Old and New Testament:{% endraw %}

{% raw %}carving. The windows are pointed, and the upper parts filled with stained{% endraw %}
{% raw %}glass. The frame-work of the roof is visible and was formerly richly or¬{% endraw %}
