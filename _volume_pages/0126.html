{% raw %}PERUGIA.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cathedral.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Italy. It consists of three admirably constructed basins, adorned{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with numerous biblical and allegorical figures in relief, executed by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Niccolb and Giovanni Pisano and Amolfo del Cambio (1280; two{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the statuettes are modern substitutes). — The W. side of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}piazza is occupied by the Episcopal Palace (PL 30), behind which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is the so-called Maesta delle Volte (PL 32), a relic of the former{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Palazzo del Podesta, which was burned down in 1329 and again in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1534.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Cathedral ofS. Lorenzo (PL 11; B, 4), dating from the 15th{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cent., is externally unfinished. Adjoining the entrance from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Piazza del Duomo is a pulpit.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The Interior, consisting of nave and aisles with a short transept, is of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}spacious but heavy dimensions. — At the beginning of each aisle is a chapel.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}On the right (of the principal entrance) is the Cappella S. Bernardino,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with a Descent from the Cross, the master-piece of Baroccio, executed in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1569; the painted window representing the Preaching of St. Bernardino{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Siena is by Constantino di Rosato and Arrigo Fiammingo of Malines,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}1565; it was restored in 1863. — On the left is the Cappella dell'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Anello , which down to 1797 contained the celebrated Sposalizio by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Perugino, now in the museum of Caen in Normandy. In both the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}chapels are beautifully carved stalls, as also in the choir. — In the Right{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Transept, a marble sarcophagus containing the remains of Popes Inno¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cent III. (d. 1216), Urban IV. (d. 1264), and Martin IV. (d. 1285). — The{% endraw %}
{% raw %}adjoining Winter-Choir contains an :;'altar-piece by Luca Signorelli: Ma¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}donna with SS. John the Baptist, Onuphrius the Hermit, Stephen, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a bishop as donor. Below the 2nd window to the left: Christ imparting{% endraw %}
{% raw %}His blessing, and saints, by Lodovico Angeli.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}In the Library are preserved precious MSS., such as the Codex of St.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Luke of the 6th cent., in gold letters on parchment.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}On the W. and N. side of the Cathedral is situated the Piazza{% endraw %}
{% raw %}del Papa (PL B, C, 3, 4) , so named from the bronze statue of Ju¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lius III. by Vine. Danti (1556). — Opposite the W. portal of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cathedral, Nos. 8-10, is the Palazzo Conestabile; the small gallery{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which it contained is now dispersed, and the celebrated Madonna by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Raphael was sold to the Emperor of Russia in 1871 for 350,000 fr.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}From the N. angle of the Piazza del Papa the Via Vecchia de¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}scends to the *Arco di Augusto (PL 2; C, 3), an ancient town-gate{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with the inscription Augusta Perusia. The foundations date from{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Etruscan period, and the upper portion from that subsequent to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the conflagration. From this point the direction of the walls of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ancient city, which occupied the height where the old part of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}present town stands, may be distinctly traced. Considerable portions{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the wall are still preserved.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The small space in front of the Arco di Augusto is called the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Piazza Gkimani (PL C, 3); to the left is the Palazzo Antinori, dating{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from 1758. —A little to the N. lies the church of S. Agostino (PI. 5 ;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}O, 2), containing several pictures by Perugino and other Umbrian{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Masters, and handsome choir-stalls.{% endraw %}
