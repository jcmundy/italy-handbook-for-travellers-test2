{% raw %}The Cathedral of S. Mustiola consists almost entirely of frag¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}The great attraction of Chiusi are the Etruscan Tombs (tickets{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of admission and guide, see above), situated in isolated hills at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}some distance from the town. The most important are the following:{% endraw %}

{% raw %}della Scimia, with mural paintings representing gladiatorial com¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bats. The Deposito del Poggio Gajelli, which is supposed, but without{% endraw %}
{% raw %}authority, to be the Mausoleum of Porsenna mentioned by Pliny and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Varro, is 3 M. distant and much dilapidated. To the N.W., the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Deposito delle Monache, 2 M.; then, to the S.E., the Deposito del{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Colle, with mural paintings, 1 M. from the town.{% endraw %}
