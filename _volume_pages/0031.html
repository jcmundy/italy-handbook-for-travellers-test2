{% raw %}Shops rarely have fixed prices. As a rule, even where prezzi{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fissi are professed, two-thirds or three-quarters of the price de¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}manded is a fair offer. The itinerant dealers often ask double and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}more the value of their wares. With artizans and drivers there is the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}same necessity for bargaining. On these occasions the expression{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'Non volete?' (then you will not?) will generally have the effect of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bringing the matter to a speedy adjustment. Purchases should never{% endraw %}
{% raw %}be made in presence of valets-de-place, as these individuals, by tacit{% endraw %}
{% raw %}agreement, receive at least 10 per cent of the purchase-money,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which of course comes out of the pocket of the purchaser.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}XI. Post Office. Telegraph.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Letters (whether 'poste restante\ Italian 'ferma in posta\ or to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the traveller's hotel) should be addressed very distinctly, and the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}name of the place should be in Italian. When asking for letters the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}traveller should present his visiting card instead of pronouncing his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}name. Postage-stamps (francobollo) are sold at the post-offices and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}at many of the tobacco-shops. — Letters of 15 grammes ('/a oz.,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}about the weight of three sous) to any of the states included in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}postal union (now comprising the whole of Europe) 30c; post-card{% endraw %}
{% raw %}(cartolina postale) 15c; book-packets per 50 grammes 7c; re¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}gistration-fee (raccomandazione) 30c.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Letters by town-post 5c; throughout the kingdom of Italy 20c{% endraw %}
{% raw %}prepaid, or 30c unpaid. Post-card 10c{% endraw %}
{% raw %}In the larger towns the post-office is open daily from 8 or 9 a.m.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to 10 p. m. (also on Sundays and holidays); in smaller places it is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}generally closed in the middle of the day for two or three hours.{% endraw %}
