{% raw %}from designs by Raphael and under his superintendence by Giulio{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Romano, Giovanni da Udine, and others of his pupils. The hall{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was originally open, and the paintings have therefore suffered se¬{% endraw %}
