{% raw %}40 M. Forli (Posta) , the ancient Forum Livii, founded by M.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Livius Salinator after the defeat of Hasdrubal, is a well-built pro¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vincial capital with 15,300 inhab. (including suburbs 38,480).{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Forli, where in 410 the marriage of Athaulf, king of the Visigoths,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with Galla Placidia, sister of the Emp. Honorius was solemnised, was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}long an independent state in which the Guelphs retained their ascendancy{% endraw %}
{% raw %}down to 1315. The Ordelaffi then usurped the supreme power, and at length",{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in 1504, Pope Julius II. annexed the city to the States of the Church. —{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Forli was the birthplace of the poet Cornelius Gallus (d. B.C. 27), of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}historian Flavio Biondo (15th cent.), and of the talented painter Melozzo{% endraw %}
{% raw %}da Forli (end of 15th cent.), who was closely allied to Piero della Fran-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cesca, and afterwards engaged at Rome.{% endraw %}
